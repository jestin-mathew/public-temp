TRENDS IN INDIA’S FINTECH SECTOR 2023:

WHAT TO LOOK FORWARD TO?

Most people usually tend to associate the term ‘artificial intelligence’ with larger-than-life machines or robots that break havoc on earth, thanks to fancy movies. However, reality chooses to differ. Rather than being a tool that brings about destruction, AI helps supplement human abilities.

Artificial intelligence refers to those systems that mimic human intelligence and help accomplish tasks that are complex for humans. However, they are not meant to replace human intelligence but to help enhance human capabilities for achieving tasks efficiently. These systems are also equipped with the ability to improve themselves based on the information they acquire. Needless to say, artificial intelligence systems are valuable for any organization. Along with other industries, AI finds use in the payment industry as well.

Today, most of us choose digital payments over cash transactions due to the convenience and speed associated with them. It has found more takers since its debut and has become people’s favourite in a matter of time. With changes in technology, the payment industry has also been evolving. One of the remarkable innovations that revamped the payment industry is artificial intelligence.

Let’s have a look at how AI is used in digital payments.

With technology changing faster, distinguishing between fraud and real transactions is an important point to be addressed. AI-based systems do a great job of processing a huge volume of transactions daily with a minimum chance of errors.

Helps prevent fraud

These systems play an important role in preventing fraudulent activities in payment gateways. They are constantly evolving to identify new patterns of fraud that may arise. AI systems use big data to determine a customer’s transaction history, analyze their user profiles and establish a pattern of their behavior. When an unusual activity occurs in the payment gateway, these systems alert the user via communication methods like text or emails.

Improves user experience

Apart from being used as a fraud detection tool, AI helps improve the speed and efficiency of payments by reducing human involvement. With the increasing volume of financial transactions, they can be processed in less time. Chatbots are common tools that are used in apps and websites to address customer queries and provide seamless services on these platforms around the clock.

Aids payment authentication

To carry out monetary transactions in a secure manner, a customer has to authenticate themselves through the appropriate methods available. Dual-factor authentication is treated as a secure method. However, with the help of AI payment gateways can provide the consumer with the ideal authentication method based on their preferences. This can range from face recognition to voice recognition.

Helps make better decisions

Processing big data is a huge task for any organization. Integration of AI into a business helps make this process more efficient and smooth such as analyzing customer profiles. This helps a business make better-informed decisions.

Increases efficiency of the system

Due to a massive volume of transactions being carried out, the need to make it faster and error-free is all the more important. These AI-based systems provide better insights and increase the processing speed of payment methods. It helps simplify processes, saving time and energy.

The usage of artificial intelligence in digital payments is set to continue in greater volumes in the future as well. Players in the financial industry could even collaborate to develop better AI systems that are mutually beneficial. The ultimate goal of all digital payment methods is to provide a safe , secure and fast transaction experience to their customers. With instant gratification being the way forward, payment providers need to team up with AI systems to deliver exceptional customer experience.

BENEFITTING FROM CHATGPT IN THE FINTECH INDUSTRY:

WHAT’S IN STORE?

When chatbots like Siri, Amazon Alexa, etc were introduced into the world, it was a fascinating concept that got many hooked. People lapped it up in no time due to its conversational abilities.

Today, the globe is buzzing about yet another exciting entrant, ChatGPT, an advanced AI-powered chatbot introduced by OpenAI, an artificial intelligence research firm. This newcomer has sparked the curiosity of many so much that “ChatGPT” is a trending topic on google search.

While many laud the usability of this chatbot, many are not quite sure how to utilize its capabilities. ChatGPT finds use in several industries like health, e-commerce, automation, marketing, etc due to its flexibility. It can be adapted to many industries and applications to suit their needs.

The financial industry can benefit tremendously from the use of Chat GPT, especially fintech. But how can Chat GPT be used in the fintech industry? Let’s find out. But before that, if you are new to ChatGPT, let’s understand it better.

What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a chatbot powered by AI that can be used to generate human-like responses for text inputs. It is created in a conversational format where it is designed to provide answers to follow-up questions, point out incorrect responses and reject inappropriate queries.

What can ChatGPT do?

Chat GPT can do the following:

  • can understand and respond to human language responses
  • generates meaningful responses based on a given input text
  • answers queries
  • has the ability to determine the sentiment behind a given text
  • can analyze and extract insights from huge amounts of data
  • used to create chatbots for various applications such as e-commerce
  • provides general knowledge on a wide range of subjects
    • How can ChatGPT be used in the fintech industry?

    Fintech organizations can help improve customer experience with the help of ChatGPT. It helps provide a superior customer experience by personalizing financial services to customers and helping them with the account setup process.

    The ability of the ChatGPT to analyze a huge volume of data can be used to detect potentially fraudulent activities in the fintech system. Also, they help speed up the loan application process by providing instant loan decisions. It analyzes customer investment data and gives personalized investment suggestions to them.

    Overall it improves efficiency and enables fintech organizations to give superior customer service.

    Advantages of using ChatGPT in fintech
    1. Helps increase efficiency
      The use of ChatGPT helps speed up financial processes by automating tasks, reducing the need for human intervention and subsequently reducing the chance for errors.
    2. Provides better decision making
      ChatGPT aids fintech firms in analyzing huge volumes of data which provides insights useful for better decision-making.
    3. Promotes time-saving
      Since ChatGPT minimizes the time needed to perform financial processes and tasks manually, it helps fintech organizations save precious time.
    4. Improves accessibility and personalized experiences
      With the help of this chatbot, financial services can be made easily accessible to the wider population and provide customers with personalized services according to their needs.
    5. Eliminates fraud
      Incorporating fraud detection systems with ChatGPT can detect potential fraud patterns, ensuring safety and preventing losses.
    Examples of successful implementations of Open AI’s GPT-3 chatbots

    ChatGPT as such hasn’t been implemented in the fintech industry yet, however, Open AI’s GPT-3 based bots have found their use in the fintech sector. Here are two examples:

  • Moneyfarm
    As an advisor platform, it provides personalized investment advice to customers. It can provide useful suggestions because it has the ability to gain a good understanding of a customer’s financial goals and risk tolerance. This has helped Moneyfarm increase its customer base and provide exceptional service.
  • N-26
    The digital bank employs GPT-3 chatbot to provide 24/7 customer service. It answers all general queries that a customer may have regarding their services reducing the need to depend on a human representative.
    • Future of ChatGPT in the fintech sector

    The fintech sector is constantly evolving with new trends and innovations arising on the horizon. Artificial intelligence is being increasingly used in fintech innovations and in no time chatbots like ChatGPT will be widely employed. Even though there are no real-life examples of successful ChatGPT implementation in fintech, the future holds great potential for this chatbot. Some possible use cases where it will add immense value are as follows:

  • Open banking
  • Regulatory developments
  • Biometric authentication
  • Insurance sector
  • Blockchain technology
  • Customer engagement and more

    • ChatGPT is a futuristic technology that is here to stay and brings revolutionary change to the fintech industry.
